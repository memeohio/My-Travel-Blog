Places You Must Visit

Stockholm,Sweden
Visited in December 2022

A small but unique Scandinavian city, Stockholm might be the perfect place for a holiday, especially in winter! With its bustling shopping center in Solna to the amazing heritage buildings in the city, what more has this to offer. I went there, and I was amaized by the nature of this city with it's clean streets and with its public transport- there's always a place in this beautiful city that you will never forget about!

Istanbul,Turkey
Visited in Summer 2022

Now that you're sitting on the couch and thinking where you would want to go on holiday. Well Istanbul makes it the perfect place for a Turkish stay. I can tell you that from my own experience, it was so good that there were no words to describe it! The food is outstanding, with its traditional doner kebeb and turkish delight! If you would want to make the most of this city, I suggest that you go to the city center, Sultanahmed. There, you can see one of the most famous islamic mosques ever made, The Blue Mosque!! It was one of the most favourite memories ever!!
